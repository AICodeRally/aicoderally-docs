# Welcome to AICodeRally Documentation

**AICodeRally** is an AI-native platform that helps people believe in AI by building something real with it.

## What is AICodeRally?

AICodeRally is a comprehensive platform with three tiers designed for different user needs:

- **Studio** - Entry-level workshop platform for learning AI development
- **Edge** - 30-day AI transformation for small businesses
- **Summit** - Enterprise AI strategy and governance platform

## Three-Tier Architecture

### Studio (Learning Platform)
Entry-level, workshop-based learning with 16+ modules covering real-world applications like birthday planners, PTA fundraisers, and more.

### Edge (SMB Platform)
30-day AI transformation for small businesses with comprehensive modules for sales, nonprofit management, and business operations.

### Summit (Enterprise Platform)
Enterprise AI strategy and governance with advanced features for large organizations.
